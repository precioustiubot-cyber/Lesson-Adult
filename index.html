Part 3: Grammar Focus

Simple Present Tense

We use the simple present tense to talk about habits, routines, and facts.

For I, you, we, they: Use the base form of the verb.

  • Example: They grow vegetables.
  • Example: We help to build the garden.

For he, she, it: Add -s to the end of the verb.

  • Example: She works in the garden.
  • Example: The garden helps the community.

Part 4: Practice Time!

A. Simple Present Practice

Complete the sentences with the correct form of the verb.

B. Correct the Sentences

Find and fix the one mistake in each sentence.

C. Write Your Own Sentences

Write two sentences about your daily routine using the simple present tense.

Part 5: Conversation Starters

  • What do you think is a good way to help your community?
  • Do you have a favorite vegetable or fruit? How often do you eat it?
  • What is one thing you do every day? Describe your daily routine.
